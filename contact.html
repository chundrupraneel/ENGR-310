FAQ

Common Questions

How soon should I start working with you?

The earlier, the better — but it's never too late. Junior year is ideal for essay brainstorming and school list building. Seniors can still benefit significantly from targeted support.

What's the difference between the AI tool and 1-on-1 sessions?

1-on-1 sessions offer personalized guidance, real-time conversation, and strategic planning. The AI tool delivers detailed instant feedback on your essays and application when you can't meet in person.

Do you work with students outside of Pittsburgh?

Yes! All sessions can be conducted virtually via video call. The AI tool is fully online and available anywhere, any time.

Can you help with financial aid even after we receive award letters?

Absolutely. I specialize in helping families understand and appeal financial aid offers — many students leave significant money on the table because they don't know they can negotiate.

How many students do you work with at a time?

I keep my client load intentionally small to ensure every student gets the personalized attention they deserve. Spots are limited, so I encourage you to reach out early.

What does the free consultation include?

A 30-minute call to discuss your goals, timeline, and current situation. I'll share my honest assessment of where you stand and how I can best help — no sales pressure, just genuine guidance.
