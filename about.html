My Philosophy

How I Approach Every Student

01

Understand Your Story

Every student has a unique background, strengths, and goals. I take the time to understand who you are before we ever touch a single essay — because the best applications tell authentic stories.

02

Build a Smart School List

I help you build a balanced, thoughtful school list — one that matches your academic profile, financial expectations, and personal fit — so every application is a realistic shot at a great outcome.

03

Craft a Compelling Application

From the Common App essay to supplements and activity descriptions, I help you present your best self in every part of your application — clearly, authentically, and memorably.

04

Maximize Your Aid

Getting in is just step one. I work with you to understand your financial aid package, identify appeal opportunities, and find scholarships that can meaningfully reduce your cost of attendance.
